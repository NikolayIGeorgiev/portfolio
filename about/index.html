Hi, I am Nikolay!

I recently (2021) graduated with a master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction and have a bachelor’s in Engineering Physics. The knowledge I have acquired throughout these two programs and from other interests affords me an interdisciplinary approach towards design – very structured, with a strong focus on the logic and flow of interaction, always keeping a in mind the real-life use cases of the design. My ultimate responsibility as a designer is to the users, the human beings we are trying to serve, and I champion their needs and experience above other requirements. I am used to conducting academic research and use that to complement best design practices.

I aim to work on meaningful projects and continue developing my skills and knowledge in UX design. Some important concepts to me are ethics and personal privacy, and I strive to learn more about how to reinforce them in my work.

In the scientific realm I am a co-author of a publication in CHI (The ACM Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems) and a blog post in ACM Interactions.

Aside from working in the digital sphere, as can be seen in my “Creative hobbies” section, I have hobbies that ground me in the physical world.
